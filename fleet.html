Lenehan Transport's Fleet

Lenehan Transport maintains a modern fleet of over 20 Volvo FH series trucks. Each truck pulls a dual temperature frigerated trailer, which allows us to carry fresh and frozen goods at the same time, to accomodate different types of shipments to suit each customer's needs. All our trucks are equipped with AdBlue, an important liquid that helps reduce harmful emissions that are created by diesel engines. It is one of the main methods that car manufacturers are using to reach strict emissions targets. We are committed to improving sustainability and AdBlue is an important factor in achieving this. Our trucks are all equipped with trackers, which allows us to accurately locate each truck, this enables us to relay that information on to our customers and update them on their deliveries in real time. Our vehicles are routinely maintained to ensure against breakdowns in transit, ensuring the deliveries can be executed without delays. Dual temperature trailers ensure the goods maintain a constant temperature in transit. These refrigerated trailers are always regularly maintained to ensure the correct product temperature .
