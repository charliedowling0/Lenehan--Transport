Lenehan Transport

Established in 1995 by Derek Lenehan, is an Irish transport company, providing temperature controlled logistics services from Ireland to Italy. We specialise in shipping chilled and frozen groupage, prodominatly meat. Our company has grown to be the home over 20 drivers serving 20 counties in Ireland and exporting to the wide expanse of Italy, from major cities to the idealic countryside.

Lenehan Transport specialises in shipping chilled and frozen groupage from Ireland to Italy.

Lenehan Transport operates and number of Volvo FH Trucks and fridgerated trailers.
